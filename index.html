Who We Are

Ancient Tradition.
Modern Voice.

Burra Katha (బుర్ర కథ) is rooted in a centuries-old Telugu performance tradition from Telangana & Andhra Pradesh — where storytellers brought epic, culturally rich tales directly to homes and local auditoriums, creating an intimate human connection.

We carry that same intimate energy into the digital world. We are a storytelling-driven media agency specializing in personal branding — positioning founders and key personnel as the living face of their brand to create unique, humanized connections that no banner or TV ad ever could.

Your audience doesn't just want to buy a product — they want to believe in a person. We make you that person.
